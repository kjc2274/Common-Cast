Welcome to Common Cast! This application allows you to search for any two movies by title, and see any cast members that appear in both films.


To use Common Cast, simply enter a movie title into each of the "Search for movie" search boxes. Once both movies are displayed, hit the big "Go!" button to see the names and pictures of any actors that appeared in both movies.
